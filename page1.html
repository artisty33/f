ABOUT US

London based company Garousca Ltd started distributing tea in the UK market in 2001. Using the experience in this sector, Garousca Ltd confidently developed a premium tea with the finest flavour, aroma, and colour to suit their clients’ desires. After years of research and development, Garousca Ltd is proud to present to the world the finest English Tea; FENJAN TEA.

We believe that our English Fenjan Tea will meet the highest consumer expectation in Europe, Middle East, and the rest of the world.

We at Fenjan Tea are always looking for companies in the Tea market, who are interested in working with us in this ever growing sector.
